About Us

ABC (Anybody Can) University, which was established in 2018, is the latest international institution of higher education in Kyoto, Japan. The campus is located in an ancient and small town called Kurama, located 15 kilometers away from Kyoto city centre. ABC University is built on 100 ha land. Despite of our young age, we have number of qualified teachers who mostly graduated from best universities in the world. We have a library with more than 1 million collection (including digital copies), computer laboratory with more than 500 computers, and we also provide dorms for students.

Main objectives of ABC University:

  • To prepare capable human resources required for the overall development of the world
  • To impart standard higher education
  • To provide education to everyone
  • To involve in extensive, empirical and timely creation of knowledge and research in the fields of science and technology
